1. Introduction

RivalScoop ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our competitive intelligence service.

This policy applies to information collected through:

  • Our website (rivalscoop.com)
  • Email communications
  • Your use of our Service
  • Other interactions with RivalScoop

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email, company name, job title
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe (we don't store card details)
  • Service Preferences: Competitors you want tracked, focus areas, delivery preferences
  • Communications: Emails, feedback, support requests

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our emails and Service
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, operating system
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication (we use minimal cookies)

2.3 Information From Third Parties

  • Payment Processors: Transaction confirmations from Stripe
  • Public Sources: Publicly available business information for our intelligence gathering

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the Service
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send weekly intelligence briefs
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support
  • Improve our Service and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Google Workspace: Email delivery
  • Web hosting providers: Service infrastructure

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to lawful requests from public authorities
  • Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Investigate potential violations of our Terms

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred with appropriate confidentiality agreements.

5. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

5.1 Right to Know

You can request information about:

  • Categories of personal information collected
  • Sources of information
  • Business purposes for collection
  • Categories of third parties with whom we share information

5.2 Right to Delete

You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.

5.3 Right to Opt-Out

We do not sell personal information, but you can opt-out of certain uses.

5.4 Right to Non-Discrimination

We won't discriminate against you for exercising privacy rights.

5.5 How to Exercise Rights

  • Email: privacy@rivalscoop.com
  • Response within 45 days
  • May request identity verification
  • Two requests per 12-month period without charge

5.6 "Do Not Sell" Notice

We DO NOT sell personal information as defined under CCPA.

5.7 Financial Incentives

Founding member pricing is not contingent on providing additional personal information.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)

  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for:
